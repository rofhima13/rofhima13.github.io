Rofhiwa (Ralph) Matumba

Rofhiwa (Ralph) Matumba is a computer scientist and record producer with a focus on practical software engineering and applied machine learning. He currently works at Powerfleet as a software developer, where he builds and maintains production systems. He is also self-employed as a record producer.

He holds a BSc in Computer Science from the University of the Western Cape and a BScHons in Computer Science from Stellenbosch University. He is currently enrolled for his Master’s degree, continuing work in data-driven systems and machine learning.
